Town One
Team #3 - Space Hamsters

Game:
Celestial Infectors

What is it:
- The player is a zombie hive mind that is trying to infect as many planets as possible while protecting your current cargo and units.

Genre:
- Strategy - Reverse Tower Defense
- Roguelike elements

Mechanics:
- Top down, isometric 2D. Player controls the overall train path, the units that are being spawned, and the direction the units are spawned.
- Each planet you conquer, you get the race as a new unit that you can use for future planets.
- There are pit stops between planets where you can enhance units.

Platform:
- PC game with keyboard/mouse and controller support.
- Touch screen controls will try and be implemented for a mobile port.

Audience:
- People that like strategy and mayhem.
